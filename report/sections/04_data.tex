\section{Data}
\label{sec:data}

Daily mean discharge for the Simme at Oberried/Lenk (GRDC station 6935331) spans
1 January 1944 to 31 December 2020 (28\,124 days; no gaps). The record was downloaded
from the Global Runoff Data Centre \citep{GRDC2026} in April 2026. Discharge ranges from
\SI{0.54}{m^3\per s} to \SI{175.0}{m^3\per s} with a daily mean of
\SI{10.82}{m^3\per s}.

GLOF event dates were compiled from three independent sources: the cantonal flood archive
\citet{GletsscherseeL2026}, the regional newspaper \citet{Simmentalzeitung2026}, and the
scientific literature \citep{Doering2021}. The year 2016 is excluded because no major
documented outburst occurred. Seven confirmed event dates are listed in
Table~\ref{tab:glof_dates}.
