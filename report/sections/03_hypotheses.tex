\section{Hypotheses}
\label{sec:hyp}

\textbf{H1 (GLOF inflation):} Fitting a GEV distribution to the full 1944--2020 annual
maximum series produces significantly larger design quantiles than fitting to the
pre-GLOF reference period (1944--2010), and the GLOF-inclusive peaks are statistically
incompatible with the reference distribution.

\textbf{H2 (Masking restores homogeneity):} After replacing the GLOF-peak contribution
with a $\pm 5$-day masked annual maximum, the 2011--2020 peaks are no longer
statistically distinguishable from the pre-GLOF reference distribution. This would imply
that the underlying natural-flood regime of the Simme has remained stationary, and that
the observed shift in extreme-value statistics is attributable solely to the direct GLOF
signal rather than to broader hydroclimatic change.
