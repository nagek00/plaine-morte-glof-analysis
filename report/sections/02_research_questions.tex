\section{Research Questions}
\label{sec:rq}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Do recurring Glacial Lake Outburst Floods (GLOFs) from the Plaine Morte glacier
        systematically distort the flood frequency distribution of the Simme river at Lenk,
        and if so, by how much do GLOF-inclusive design quantiles exceed those from a
        homogeneous reference period?

  \item Are the annual maximum discharges recorded during the GLOF period (2011--2020)
        statistically consistent with the pre-GLOF reference distribution once the direct
        GLOF contribution is removed, or does the post-2010 natural-flood regime show
        evidence of additional change?
\end{enumerate}
